<p><ul><li>Standing Skyline</li><li>Running Skyline</li><li>Live Skyline</li><li>High Lead</li><li>Jammer & Tong Thrower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Yarders are crucial in logging operations, with various market types reflecting their operational methods. Standing Skyline systems anchor a cable above the ground, reducing forest impact. Running Skyline uses moving cables for more flexible log transport. Live Skyline involves a continuously tensioned cable, enhancing efficiency. High Lead systems utilize a tall spar tree for greater reach. Jammer systems employ a machine to lift logs, while Tong Throwers use clamping mechanisms to grip and maneuver logs, each designed to optimize timber extraction.</p></p>
