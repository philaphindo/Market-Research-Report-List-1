<p><p>The Dry Mix Mortar Additives market comprises various types designed to enhance performance and workability. Redispersible Polymer Powders improve adhesion and flexibility. Plasticizers enhance fluidity and workability, allowing for easier application. Defoamers eliminate air bubbles, ensuring a smooth finish. Cellulose Ethers provide improved water retention and flow. Air Entraining Agents introduce tiny air pockets, enhancing durability and thermal insulation. Other additives might include accelerators or retarders, further customizing mortar properties for specific applications.</p></p>
