AR glasses overlay digital information onto the real world, enabling users to interact with both environments simultaneously. VR glasses provide immersive experiences by completely blocking the real world and immersing users in a virtual environment. MR glasses combine elements of both AR and VR, allowing for interaction with real and virtual objects in real-time, enhancing user engagement and application versatility.</p></p>
