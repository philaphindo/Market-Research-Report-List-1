<p><p>The non-reflective glass market can be divided into two main types: chemical treatment and physical processing. Chemical treatment involves applying specialized coatings through various chemical methods to reduce glare and reflections, enhancing visibility. On the other hand, physical processing includes techniques like sandblasting or etching the glass surface to achieve a non-reflective finish. Both approaches aim to improve optical performance while catering to different applications, such as in displays, architecture, and automotive sectors, enhancing aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
