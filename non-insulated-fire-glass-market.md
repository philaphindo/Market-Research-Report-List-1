<p><p>The Non-insulated Fire Glass market consists primarily of two types: Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass and Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass. Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass is a single layer of tempered glass designed to withstand high temperatures, providing transparency and fire resistance. In contrast, Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass combines multiple layers or materials to enhance strength and thermal performance while maintaining clarity. Both types are essential for applications requiring safety and visibility in environments exposed to flames or high heat, such as commercial and industrial spaces.</p></p>
