<p><ul><li>Steam Distillation</li><li>Cold Pressed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Betel Leaf Essential Oil market consists of two primary extraction methods: steam distillation and cold pressing. Steam distillation utilizes heated water to vaporize the oil, separating it from the leaf through condensation, yielding a high-quality oil with pure aroma and therapeutic properties. In contrast, cold pressing mechanically extracts the oil without heat, preserving the leaf's natural compounds and flavor. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications in aromatherapy, cosmetics, and traditional medicine, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
