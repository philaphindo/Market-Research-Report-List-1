<p><p>The customer display market primarily includes two types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and VFD (Vacuum Fluorescent Display). LCDs are popular for their vibrant visuals and versatility, often used in modern retail environments due to their energy efficiency and slim design. In contrast, VFDs provide bright, clear characters and are favored for their excellent readability in various lighting conditions. Both technologies cater to different customer needs, influencing purchasing decisions and enhancing point-of-sale interactions. Their choice depends on specific application requirements and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
