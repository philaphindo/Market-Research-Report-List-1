<p><strong>The Pressure Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Spring tube</li><li>Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure relay market comprises various types, including plunger, diaphragm, spring tube, and bellows relays. Plunger relays use a mechanical plunger to activate switches based on pressure changes, ensuring precise control. Diaphragm relays utilize a flexible diaphragm that responds to pressure variations, offering sensitivity and reliability. Spring tube relays employ a spring mechanism for detecting pressure levels, providing straightforward performance. Bellows relays feature a bellows structure that expands or contracts with pressure fluctuations, enabling effective control in diverse applications.</p></p>
