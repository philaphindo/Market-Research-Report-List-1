<p><p>The panel solar simulator market is categorized into three main types: Continuous Type, Flashed Type, and Pulsed Type. Continuous Type simulators provide a steady light output, ideal for long-duration testing. Flashed Type simulators emit short bursts of light, replicating high-intensity sunlight for brief periods, useful for assessing panel response under peak conditions. Pulsed Type simulators combine both continuous and pulsed methods, allowing for versatile testing scenarios. Each type serves specific testing needs, enhancing efficiency in solar panel evaluation and research.</p></p>
