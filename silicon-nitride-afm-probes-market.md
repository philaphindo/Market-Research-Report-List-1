<p><p>Silicon nitride AFM probes are designed for atomic force microscopy applications and are available in two main types: coated and uncoated. Coated probes feature a thin layer of additional material, enhancing their mechanical properties and reducing wear, making them suitable for diverse samples and environments. Uncoated probes, on the other hand, maintain the inherent properties of silicon nitride, offering superior imaging resolution and stability for sensitive measurements. Both types cater to specific research and industrial needs, balancing cost, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>
