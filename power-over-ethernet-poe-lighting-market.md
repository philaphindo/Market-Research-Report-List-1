<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) lighting market consists of two key types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and Integrated Circuits (ICs), and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers are responsible for managing power delivery via Ethernet cables to connected devices, while PD controllers ensure that powered devices, like PoE-enabled LED lights, receive the appropriate power level for operation. Both components are crucial for creating efficient, scalable, and adaptable lighting solutions in smart buildings and infrastructure.</p></p>
