<p><p>The grain sorting machine market comprises three main types: Optical Sorting Machines, Gravity/Weight Sorting Machines, and Other Machines. Optical sorting machines utilize advanced imaging technology to detect and separate grains based on color, size, and shape, ensuring high purity and quality. Gravity/Weight sorting machines rely on the differences in weight and density to separate grains, making them effective for sorting bulk materials. Other machines may include manual or semi-automated systems designed for specific grain types or smaller operations, enhancing overall sorting efficiency.</p></p>
