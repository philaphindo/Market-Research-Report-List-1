<p><strong>The Photographic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Pass Filter</li><li>Short Pass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photographic filter market includes various types, primarily long pass and short pass filters. Long pass filters transmit wavelengths longer than a specific cutoff, effectively blocking shorter wavelengths, which enhances colors and reduces haze in photography. Conversely, short pass filters transmit shorter wavelengths while blocking longer ones, enhancing contrast and saturation for specific effects. Both filters are widely used in digital and analog photography, allowing photographers to manipulate light and achieve desired visual outcomes in their images.</p></p>
