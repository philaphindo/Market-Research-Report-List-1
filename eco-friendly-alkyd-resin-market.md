<p><ul><li>Long Oil Alkyd (LOA) Containing 56% Fatty Acids</li><li>Medium Oil Alkyd (MOA) Containing 46-55% Fatty Acids</li><li>Short Oil Alkyd (SOA) Containing Less Than 35-45% Fatty Acids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly alkyd resin market is categorized into three main types based on oil length and fatty acid content. Long Oil Alkyd (LOA), containing 56% fatty acids, offers excellent flexibility and gloss, making it suitable for outdoor applications. Medium Oil Alkyd (MOA), with 46-55% fatty acids, provides a balance between durability and drying time, ideal for general-purpose coatings. Short Oil Alkyd (SOA), containing less than 35-45% fatty acids, dries quickly and is often used in high-performance industrial applications.</p></p>
