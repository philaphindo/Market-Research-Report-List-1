<p><p>The AI chips market comprises several types, each suited for different applications. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in parallel processing, making them ideal for AI workloads like neural networks. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-built for specific tasks, offering high efficiency and performance for dedicated AI applications. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) provide flexibility, allowing reconfiguration for various tasks, balancing performance and adaptability. CPUs (Central Processing Units), while versatile, are less optimized for heavy AI computations compared to the other specialized types.</p></p>
