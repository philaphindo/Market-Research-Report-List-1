<p><p>The polyester board market includes three main types: Polyester MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard), which combines wood fibers with polyester resins for enhanced durability and moisture resistance; Polyester Plywood, known for its layered construction and superior surface finish, ideal for furniture and cabinetry; and Polyester Blockboard, which consists of a core of wooden strips sandwiched between veneer layers, offering strength and stability. Each type is popular for applications in furniture manufacturing and interior design, emphasizing aesthetic appeal and functionality.</p></p>
