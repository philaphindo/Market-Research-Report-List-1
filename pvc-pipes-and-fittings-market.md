<p><strong>The PVC Pipes and Fittings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>K Type</li><li>L Type</li><li>M Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC pipes and fittings are categorized into three main market types: K Type, L Type, and M Type. K Type pipes are thick-walled, providing high strength and durability, suitable for high-pressure applications. L Type pipes have a medium wall thickness, balancing cost and performance, often used for water supply systems. M Type pipes feature thinner walls, making them lightweight and economical, ideal for low-pressure applications. Each type caters to different functional requirements and construction standards in plumbing and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
