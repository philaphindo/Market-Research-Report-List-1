<p><strong>The Offshore Wind Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis</li><li>Vertical Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal axis and vertical axis turbines. Horizontal axis turbines are the most common, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal axis to harness wind energy efficiently. They are typically taller and have larger rotor diameters, making them suitable for high wind areas. Vertical axis turbines, on the other hand, have blades that rotate around a vertical axis and are often more compact, allowing for easier installation and maintenance in more varied marine environments.</p></p>
