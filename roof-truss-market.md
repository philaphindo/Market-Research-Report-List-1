<p><p>The roof truss market includes various types designed for specific structural and aesthetic needs. **Attic Trusses** create usable attic space, ideal for storage or additional rooms. **Gable Trusses** form triangular roof ends, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for standard roofs. **Scissor Trusses** feature sloping bottom chords, offering vaulted ceilings and enhanced interior aesthetics. **Others** encompass specialized trusses like mono, hip, or gambrel, catering to unique architectural designs. Each type balances functionality, space utilization, and visual appeal, meeting diverse construction requirements.</p></p>
