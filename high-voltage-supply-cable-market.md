<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Voltage Supply Cable market is categorized into two main types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode. Single Mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission, utilizing a single light path for minimal signal loss, making them ideal for high-capacity applications. In contrast, Multi-Mode cables employ multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distance transmissions at lower costs, suited for local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct requirements within high voltage power distribution and telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
