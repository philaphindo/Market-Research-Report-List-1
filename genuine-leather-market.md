<p><p>The genuine leather market comprises various types, notably top-grain and split leather. Top-grain leather, the highest quality, is made from the upper layer of hide, retaining its natural texture while being buffed for a smoother finish, making it durable and luxurious. Split leather, derived from the lower layers, is less expensive and often treated to enhance appearance but lacks the strength and texture of top-grain. Together, these types cater to diverse consumer preferences, balancing quality and affordability in leather products.</p></p>
